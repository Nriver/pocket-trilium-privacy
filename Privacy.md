Privacy Policy

Thank you for using our application! Your privacy is very important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information while you use our app. Below is our privacy policy, designed to help you understand how we handle your data.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Our app does not require you to provide any private information.

2. Use of Storage Permission

In order for the app to function properly, it may request access to your device's storage. This permission is solely used for the following purposes:

To persistently store your note data on your device.

To import note files from your personal computer.

We do not access or store any of your personal files or any content stored on your device, unless it is part of an operation you explicitly authorize.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app does not use any third-party analytics tools, advertising services, or other third-party integrations. Therefore, your data will not be accessed or used by any external service.

4. Data Security

Although we do not collect any personal data, we still encourage you to ensure that your usage environment is secure. Please refrain from sharing your device access credentials and passwords to prevent unauthorized access to your data.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect our latest privacy practices. When we make updates to the privacy policy, we will notify you within the app or in another appropriate manner.
